REMINDERS

In exams with multiple choice questions, often there is not much time for each question, for example one to two minutes. It’s therefore important to be efficient with your time.

During the exam, choose the best /correct answer. Don’t spend a long time on a question if the meaning is confusing or you don’t know the answer. Instead, move on and return to the difficult questions at the end. And also, a wrong answer is worth the same as no answer (no marks). In that case, it’s better to guess than to leave a question blank.
